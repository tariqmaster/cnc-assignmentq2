LAM SERIES:

General purpose lamination inks for Gravure process. Suitable for PET+BOPP; BOPP+MOPP; PET+MCPP & PET+LDPE (up to 50micron PE) structures etc.

X-LAM SERIES:

General purpose lamination inks for Gravure process with good color strength. Suitable for PET+BOPP; BOPP+MOPP; PET+MCPP & PET+LDPE (up to 50micron PE) structures etc.

GLITZ SERIES:

Toluene based lamination inks for Gravure Process. Suitable for PET+BOPP; BOPP+MOPP; PET+MCPP structures etc.

MAX SERIES:

Surface/Top printing Toluene based low cost ink for Gravure Process. Suitable for BOPP, MOPP, MPET, PET, LDPE.

PRIME SERIES:

Surface/Top printing, high performance inks for Gravure process. Suitable for BOPP, MOPP, MPET, PET, LDPE.

PEL SERIES:

Ink for Pearlized OPP for deep freeze ice cream jobs; suitable for Gravure surface printing. Good scratch/ rub resistance.

DELUXE SERIES:

High performance Lamination inks for Gravure process. Suitable for PET+BOPP; BOPP+MOPP; PET+MCPP & PET+LDPE (Up to 150micron LDPE) structures etc.

PIVOTAL SERIES:

Polyamide based inks for flexo process. Suitable for high quality PE bags. Excellent gloss, ink transfer, good scratch/rub resistance and low COF.

PEAK SERIES:

Ink for high speed central impression flexo machines. Excellent transfer and dot sharpness. Good Scratch/rub resistance, low COF. Suitable for ghee films.

PVC SERIES:

Ink for PVC sleeves and twist PVC equally good for imported or local available PVC.

SUPER SERIES:

SUPER is a Toluene free printing ink series for reverse gravure lamination on variety of flexible films using good quality solvent based and solvent less adhesives. These inks are used for high profile lamination jobs especially for triple laminated structures.

NC BASED PASTE:

We have good quality of NC pastes, all major colors are available in paste form.

POLYAMIDE BASED CONCENTRATE:

We have alcohol soluble polyamide based concentrates with good color strength and gloss.

TECHNICAL VARNISHES:

Wide range of Technical Varnishes are available for different types of applications, including nearly all Gravure and Flexo applications.

OVER PRINT VARNISHES:

OPVs play a vital role in top printing applications to improve appeal and performance of inks on the printed substrate. We have good range of OPVs for different applications.
